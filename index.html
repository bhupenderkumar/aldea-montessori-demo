Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program serves children aged 2-3, providing a gentle introduction to Montessori principles and a love of learning.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves children in grades 1-6, offering a rigorous and engaging curriculum that prepares students for success in middle school and beyond.

🌟

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves children in grades 7-8, providing a bridge between Elementary and High School, with a focus on academic rigor, character development, and community service.
