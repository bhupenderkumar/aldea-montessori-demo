Our Mission & History

Aldea Montessori was founded in 2010 with a vision to provide a nurturing, child-centered education in Phoenix. Our campus is designed to inspire exploration and creativity, with a focus on outdoor learning and community engagement.

Our Montessori program is built on the principles of self-directed learning, hands-on activity, and collaborative play. Our experienced teachers guide students as they develop essential skills in reading, writing, math, and critical thinking.

In addition to academic excellence, we prioritize character development and extracurricular activities that foster social responsibility, teamwork, and emotional intelligence. Our students are encouraged to pursue their passions and interests outside of the classroom.
